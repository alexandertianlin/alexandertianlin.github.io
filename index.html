About Me

I am a Master's student at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, researching multi-modal hand tracking fusion for dexterous robotic manipulation. My work combines 3D hand mesh recovery (HaMeR), pose estimation (ViTPose), IMU glove sensor fusion, and RealSense depth cameras to build real-time human hand-to-robot retargeting pipelines. I am currently preparing a research paper on per-finger contact-event tactile world-action models with arm-hand coordination VLA.

Previously, I built AI-driven systems ranging from LLM Agent workflows to IEEE award-winning ML healthcare models. I thrive at the intersection of computer vision, robotics, embedded systems, and AI.
